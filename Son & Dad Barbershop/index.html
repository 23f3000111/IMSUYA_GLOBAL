Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Precision

Son & Dad Barbershop is more than just a place for a haircut — it's a heritage of grooming passed down through generations. Located in the heart of George Town, Penang, and now expanded to Selangor, we blend classic barbering techniques with modern style.

When you know how to truly see a haircut, you'll never look at the world in the same way again. Every cut tells a story, every shave is a craft.
