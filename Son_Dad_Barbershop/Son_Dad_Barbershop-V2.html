THE HOT TOWEL SHAVE
EXPERIENCE

Indulge in our most celebrated service — a ritual that blends old-world craftsmanship with modern luxury. Every hot towel shave is a journey from tension to total relaxation.

01

PREPARATION

A steaming hot towel is applied to open pores and soften facial hair, preparing your skin for the smoothest shave possible.

02

PREMIUM LATHER

Rich, warm shaving cream is applied with a traditional brush, creating a protective layer for an irritation-free experience.

03

THE SHAVE

Our expert barbers use precise straight razor techniques — smooth, confident strokes for a flawlessly close shave.

04

COOL & SOOTHE

A refreshing cool towel closes pores, followed by premium aftershave balm to hydrate and protect your skin.
