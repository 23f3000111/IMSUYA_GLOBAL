About Roadtainers

FROM INITIATION
TO COMPLETION

Roadtainers (Mombasa) Limited is a privately owned heavy commercial transport company. With clear goals and strong leadership, we have become Kenya's most efficient transport operator.

We handle heavy commercial goods, oil products and tanker transport — delivering professional service across all of East Africa.

  • Operating 7 days a week, Monday through Sunday
  • 1,800 m² warehouse & transit yard in Mombasa
  • Fully equipped in-house vehicle workshop
  • Certified experience across East & Central Africa
