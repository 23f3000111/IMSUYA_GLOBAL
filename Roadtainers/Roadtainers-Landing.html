Who We Are

East Africa's Most
Trusted Transport
Company

Roadtainers (Mombasa) Ltd is a privately-owned company dealing in heavy commercial transport. From our 18-acre, fully secured facility — just 3.5 km from the Port of Mombasa — we deliver logistics solutions built on over two decades of excellence and a Gold Medal for Business in Excellence.

  • Headquarters on the Mombasa-Nairobi Highway — 10 km from Mombasa City
  • Liaison office in Kampala, Uganda for regional coverage
  • Fleet of 140+ Mercedes Benz trucks — GPS & ECTS tracked
  • Winner — Trade Leaders Club International Transport Award

What We Offer

Comprehensive Transport
& Logistics Solutions

From heavy cargo transport to precision lifting, secured transit facilities and a full in-house workshop — we cover every aspect of your logistics needs.

Heavy Transport

GPS/ECTS-tracked fleet of 140+ trucks. We import our own tyres and spares with an in-house fueling facility — maximum uptime guaranteed.

Tractor Trailers

Tippers, oil tankers, low loaders up to 135 tons for Oil & Gas, flatbeds, sided trailers and box body trailers — fully equipped for any cargo.

Lifting Facilities

Three 50-ton cranes, one 30-ton crane plus forklifts up to 13 tons. Precision lifting engineered for the most demanding cargo requirements.

Transit Facilities

18-acre fully secured facility, 3.5 km from port. Handles exports & imports with a resident customs officer on-site for fast clearance.

General Cargo

End-to-end logistics and transport solutions. Successfully providing comprehensive logistics & transport services across East Africa.

In-House Workshop

Full-fledged workshop handling engine & gearbox maintenance, trailer construction & repair, chassis repair — keeping our fleet in prime condition.
